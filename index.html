About us

    •Amol Electricals is a family-owned business founded by Mohan Bhagwan Yadav in 1995, with a focus on electrical motor rewinding services. Over the years, we have expanded our services to include the establishment of new motors and all types of agriculture water pump spare parts as well, providing comprehensive solutions for all our clients' electrical needs.

    •Our success is driven by a combination of hard work, dedication, and a commitment to customer satisfaction. We take pride in our ability to provide customized solutions for our clients, leveraging our years of expertise and knowledge in the markets to deliver the best possible outcomes.

    •Amol Electricals specializes in a wide range of electrical services, including motor rewinding, installation of new motors, and agricultural water pump spare parts. Our team of experienced technicians has a deep understanding of electrical systems, circuits, and components, enabling us to deliver high-quality solutions to our clients.
